Author: Nguyen Chi Bao - B2012178

Speed: x2

Specific information

DFS(u, visited, graph)

   u.visited = true (color the vertex u)

   if u is the target vertex, found, exit

   for (let v of graph[u])

      if (!visited[v])

         DFS(v, visited, graph);
